A full feedback / idea-board screen: a titled header with a "Submit idea" action,
a list of idea cards (vote tally, title, status pill, author, relative timestamp,
comment count), and pagination. Use as a layout template for browsing and voting
on feedback. Composed from `Button`, `UserName`, `Moment`, and `Pagination`. Takes
no props — render `<FeedbackBoard />`.
